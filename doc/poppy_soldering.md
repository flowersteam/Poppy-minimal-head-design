# Poppy-humanoid soldering tutorial

This tutorial explain step by step how to procude custom wire needed to your poppy-humanoid.

You will need:

  - Audio Jack connector
  - Screw terminal connector 2P
  - Screw terminal connector 4P
  - Audio cable
  - Large heat shrink
  - Small heat shrink
  - Molex connector 4P
  - Contact Crimping
  - Black/red Cable
